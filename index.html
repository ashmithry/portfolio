DECA Forge

Role: Project Lead, Tech Team Manager

Technology:

"Building a comprehensive management platform to automate operations for a 200:1 student-to-staff ratio in our DECA chapter."

Problem

As our DECA chapter grew 300% to over 500 members, our 10-person student leadership team was overwhelmed. We faced bottlenecks in communication, event registration, and tracking member participation, all of which were handled manually through spreadsheets and email.

Solution

I initiated and led the development of "DECA Forge," a custom web platform to centralize all chapter operations. The platform aims to automate event sign-ups, track participation points, and provide a central communication hub for all members, effectively solving our 200:1 student-staff operational challenge.
